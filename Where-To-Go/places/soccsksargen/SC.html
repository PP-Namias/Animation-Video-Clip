Welcome to South Cotabato

The province, known as the "Land of the Dreamweavers," is situated in the southern region of Mindanao. It is renowned for its exceptional craft of T'nalak weaving, which has become a cultural symbol of the province. T'nalak weaving is a traditional art form passed down through generations, with T'boli women playing a significant role in its creation.

Using colored abaca fibers, these skilled weavers transform their dreams into reality by meticulously crafting intricate and vibrant patterns. The geometric designs found in T'nalak reflect the rich cultural heritage and artistic expression of the T'boli people.

The T'nalak weaving tradition has not only preserved the indigenous cultural identity of the province but has also become a source of livelihood for many T'boli communities. Visitors to the province can witness the craftsmanship firsthand and appreciate the beauty and significance of T'nalak as a tangible representation of the province's cultural heritage.
