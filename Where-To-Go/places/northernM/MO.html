Welcome to Misamis Oriental

Misamis Oriental offers an abundance of thrilling adventures and captivating natural wonders that are sure to satisfy any adrenaline seeker. Its unique geological and climatic conditions have given rise to a diverse landscape, encompassing towering mountains, lush jungles, picturesque beaches, meticulously terraced hills, and remarkable cultural landmarks. Visitors to this province can expect an array of choices that will keep them captivated, providing an unforgettable experience filled with excitement, contentment, and a sense of adventure.

One exciting destination in Misamis Oriental is the Lasang Secret Adventure Park, located in Initao. This park offers a thrilling jungle adventure, immersing visitors in the beauty of the woodlands and providing an unforgettable experience. Another noteworthy attraction is the Initao-Libertad Protected Landscape & Seascape, often referred to as the "Forest by the Sea." This unique environment boasts a lush limestone forest and is home to diverse wildlife. Additionally, its coastline serves as a nesting site for endangered Marine Turtles, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts.

For those interested in underwater exploration, the coral gardens of Misamis Oriental and Duka Bay Resort in Medina offer exceptional scuba diving opportunities. Duka Bay Resort is renowned as the best scuba diving site in the province, allowing visitors to discover the vibrant marine life beneath the surface. While en route to Medina, a stopover in Jasaan is highly recommended for a refreshing experience in the "Town of Springs and Falls." Here, visitors can indulge in the invigorating waters cascading from the mountainside or relax on the municipality's expansive beach while enjoying breathtaking scenic views.

Misamis Oriental is truly a paradise for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers, promising an endless array of thrilling activities and awe-inspiring sights that will leave visitors with unforgettable memories.
