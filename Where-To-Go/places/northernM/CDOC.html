Welcome to Cagayan De Oro City

Cagayan de Oro City serves as the hub for business, trade, information technology, and adventure tourism in Mindanao. It has become a prominent convention center for Visayas and Mindanao, offering various facilities for hosting conventions and similar events. Being known as the "City of Golden Opportunities," it is recognized as the regional growth capital of Northern Mindanao, attracting investments in the area. Additionally, it serves as a crucial transshipment point for agricultural and industrial products from nearby provinces.

Visitors to Cagayan de Oro City can explore several interesting attractions, including the General Douglas McArthur Marker, Lim Ket Kai Centre, Del Monte Philippines Cannery, Plaza Divisoria, Museum of Three Cultures of Capitol University, La Castilla Museum of Liceo de Cagayan University, and Xavier University Folkloric Museum.

The city offers a diverse range of accommodation options, catering to all types of visitors, from budget to standard to luxury hotels. When it comes to dining and entertainment, Cagayan de Oro City is renowned as the culinary center of Mindanao. Local establishments such as Kagay-anon Restaurant, Thai Me Up, and Pryce Plaza Hotel have gained national recognition. Visitors can indulge in unique culinary experiences, including ostrich meat, premium steaks, Lechon de Leche, and the famous "Pigok" fish, a seasonal delicacy found in the Cagayan de Oro River.

Spas are readily available throughout the city, providing relaxation and rejuvenation options. Shopping enthusiasts will find a wide array of items in various shopping malls, gift and souvenir shops, and specialty stores. Ethnic-made products like Kamuyot or Mindanao Silk, native bags, decorations, furniture, clay pots, and world-class stoneware products are among the many choices available for purchase.
