Welcome to Ilocos Sur

Ilocos Sur offers the delightful experience of exploring the charming town of Vigan, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Stepping into Vigan feels like embarking on a journey back in time, as the Spanish-era houses have been meticulously preserved throughout the years. Walking through its streets allows you to immerse yourself in the rich history and architectural beauty of the past.

As you wander through Vigan, be sure to indulge in the town's unique delicacies. One must-try specialty is the Vigan longganisa, a delicious local sausage with its distinct flavors. Another mouthwatering treat is the Ilocos empanada, a savory pastry filled with a combination of ingredients such as ground meat, vegetables, and egg. These culinary delights are signature dishes of the region and offer a delightful gastronomic experience.

Exploring Vigan's streets, savoring its delicacies, and admiring its well-preserved Spanish-era houses make for an unforgettable visit to Ilocos Sur.
