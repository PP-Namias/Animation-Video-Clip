Welcome to Taguig

The City of Taguig has transformed into a thriving urban landscape and has emerged as a highly urbanized city. It is rapidly becoming the country's next center for investment, finance, and commerce. One of its prominent development areas is the Bonifacio Global City (BGC), spanning 240 hectares. BGC is a dynamic financial and lifestyle district that boasts a wide range of amenities and attractions.

In BGC, you will find a selection of international hotel chains, upscale malls, restaurants, and cafes catering to various tastes and preferences. The district is also home to the country's first world-class science museum, offering a unique educational experience. Additionally, BGC features a franchise of a popular Mexican chain of family and entertainment centers, providing a vibrant and lively atmosphere for residents and visitors.

The planning and development of Taguig City, particularly in BGC, prioritize well-designed landscapes, efficient traffic systems, pocket parks, and wide pedestrian sidewalks. These elements contribute to creating a community haven that fosters a sense of connectivity and livability.

The combination of modern infrastructure, commercial opportunities, recreational facilities, and a well-designed urban environment make BGC and the City of Taguig an attractive destination for urban dwellers. The city offers a vibrant and dynamic lifestyle that caters to the needs and preferences of its residents and visitors alike.
