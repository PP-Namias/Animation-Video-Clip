Welcome to Bataan

Experience the enchantment of Bataan, a captivating historical and cultural region located just an hour away from Manila by ferryboat across the bay. While it may not have been a traditional tourist destination in the past, Bataan has now emerged as a haven for travelers, offering a plethora of exciting places to visit and a wide array of interesting activities to engage in. This province is a treasure trove of culture, natural beauty, historical sites, pristine beaches, and the warm hospitality that is quintessentially Filipino.

Bataan is composed of several municipalities including Abucay, Bagac, Dinalupihan, Hermosa, Limay, Mariveles, Morong, Orani, Orion, Pilar, and Samal. The component city of Balanga and the Bataan Freeport Zone also add to the region's diverse offerings.

Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Bataan as you explore its historical sites and landmarks. Delve into the province's significant role in Philippine history, particularly during World War II, by visiting historical sites such as the Bataan Death March Memorial Shrine and the Mount Samat National Shrine. These locations provide a glimpse into the past and pay tribute to the bravery and sacrifice of the Filipino soldiers.

Bataan also boasts stunning natural beauty, including picturesque beaches that are perfect for relaxation and water activities. Unwind on the pristine shores, bask in the sun, and enjoy the serene ambiance. The province's warm and inviting waters are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and diving.

In addition to its historical and natural attractions, Bataan offers a range of exciting activities. Embark on an adventure-filled hike through the trails of Mount Natib or join an eco-tour to discover the province's diverse flora and fauna. Engage in water sports such as kayaking or jet skiing, or go on a thrilling boat ride to explore the coastal areas and nearby islands.

Bataan is also renowned for its warm hospitality and friendly locals. Immerse yourself in the local culture and interact with the residents, who are known for their warm smiles and genuine kindness.

Whether you are seeking cultural immersion, natural wonders, historical insights, or simply a relaxing beach getaway, Bataan has it all. Discover the magic of this remarkable province, where culture, beauty, history, unspoiled beaches, and Filipino hospitality converge.
