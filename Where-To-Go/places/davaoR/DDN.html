Welcome to Davao Del Norte

Davao del Norte is renowned as an agricultural province, earning its title as the Banana Capital of the Philippines due to its thriving banana plantations managed by both multinational corporations and local producers. However, beyond its agricultural significance, the province offers a wide range of tourist attractions that cater to various interests.

For beach enthusiasts and adventure seekers, the Island Garden City of Samal is a must-visit destination. It is home to stunning diving sites and picturesque beaches that promise unforgettable experiences. Whether you're looking to explore vibrant marine life underwater or simply relax on pristine shores, Samal offers a haven for beach bums and nature lovers alike.

Moreover, the capital city of Tagum is experiencing rapid infrastructure development, which has led to the rise of MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conventions, and Exhibitions) and sports tourism in the province. With its modern facilities and amenities, Tagum has become an attractive destination for hosting conferences, exhibitions, and sports events.

While Davao del Norte's agricultural sector remains its primary strength, the province's tourism offerings are gaining recognition. Visitors can enjoy the best of both worlds by exploring the agricultural landscapes and experiencing the natural wonders and recreational opportunities that the province has to offer.
