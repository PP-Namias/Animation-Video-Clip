About Baguio City

Destination

Welcome to Baguio City

Baguio, which is known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, is the best place to travel if you want to escape the tropical heat in the lowlands. The city rarely experiences temperatures higher than 26°C even during the warmest times of the year, with typical temperatures ranging from 15 to 23°C. Baguio is also home to lovely plants that wouldn't often survive elsewhere in the country due to its climate. You'll be in for a treat with all the lovely flowers and lush flora as you enter the area, which is also known as the City of Pines. You'll genuinely feel as though you're stepping into an other universe.

See And Do

The area has a lot more to offer than just a location to cool off and enjoy the cold, including unique food and souvenirs as well as an abundance of flora and lovely parks. Visit the Baguio Cathedral, Camp John Hay, and Burnham Park, among of the city's most recognizable sites. In Baguio's several neighborhoods, you can choose to shop to your heart's delight, be one with nature, or ride a bike or boat.Additionally, you can visit a strawberry field to choose some fresh fruit and perhaps even sample strawberry taho! This mountain resort city in Northern Luzon is easily reachable by bus or automobile, making it a popular destination for impromptu road excursions. Explore Baguio to find out what awaits you and why both domestic and international tourists include it on their bucket lists of places to visit in the Philippines.

Where To Go

There are many homestays in Baguio that may accommodate any budget, from opulent hotels to moderately priced temporary homes. You won't be missing out on any of the excitement in the city because the majority of these selections are widely accessible and close to the most popular tourist attractions in the region.
