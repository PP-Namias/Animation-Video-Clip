Welcome to Southern Leyte

Southern Leyte is a province that captivates like a beautiful lady. Its external beauty is evident, but it is the hidden gems within that make it truly enchanting. Immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters of Sogod Bay and witness the breathtaking beauty of its depths. Sogod Bay is home to one of the most diverse and unspoiled reefs in the Philippines, boasting steep drop-offs and vibrant reefs teeming with marine life. Dive into the blue waters and interact with the locals' beloved "TIKI-TIKI," the gentle whale sharks. Experience the colors and tranquility of the ocean while exploring the historic Limasawa Island. Delve into the rich culture of the Pintados by visiting the pre-colonial museum "BALANGHAI MASUA," where you can learn about "BAYBAYIN" writing and Pintado tattooing. Playfully interact with the schools of fish that curiously nibble at your fingers and be awestruck by the majestic marine world.

After a delightful dive, take a leisurely stroll and discover Southern Leyte's finest restaurants. Indulge in mouthwatering Banagan (lobsters) in Silago, savor the crispy Ampao in Pintuyan, and satisfy your sweet tooth with Bocarillo in Maasin City. Embark on a spelunking adventure in Cambaro Caves and explore the serene mangroves of Sta. Cruz - Molopolo in Macrohon, where you can encounter black-tipped sharks and the rare Philippine Wild Ducks. Southern Leyte keeps its secrets hidden, only revealing them to those who take the time to truly get to know and appreciate its wonders.
