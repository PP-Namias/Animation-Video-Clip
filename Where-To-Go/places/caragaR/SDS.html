About Surigao Del Sur

Destination

Welcome to Surigao Del Sur

Located in the northeastern coast of Mindanao, our province is naturally advantaged and offers a wealth of natural beauty and attractions. Stretching approximately 300 kilometers in length and 50 kilometers at its widest stretch, Surigao del Sur is a stunning destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

To the northwest, we are bounded by the province of Surigao del Norte, while Davao Oriental lies to our southeast. The majestic Pacific Ocean graces our eastern shores, providing breathtaking views and opportunities for coastal activities. On the west and southwest, we are bordered by the provinces of Agusan del Norte and Agusan del Sur. The Diwata Mountain Range stands proudly along our northwestern boundaries, separating us from the rest of Mindanao.

With our coastal location, Surigao del Sur boasts picturesque beaches and pristine waters. It is a haven for beach enthusiasts, water sports lovers, and those seeking relaxation by the sea. Dive into the crystal-clear waters, explore vibrant coral reefs, or simply bask in the sun on our sandy shores.

But Surigao del Sur is not just about its coastline. Our province is also blessed with diverse landscapes, including rugged mountains and lush forests. The Diwata Mountain Range offers opportunities for hiking, trekking, and immersing oneself in the beauty of nature. Marvel at cascading waterfalls, discover hidden caves, or navigate the meandering rivers that flow through our province.

We invite you to explore our natural wonders, such as the enchanting Tinuy-an Falls, known as the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines," with its multi-tiered cascades and lush surroundings. Visit Hinatuan Enchanted River, a mystical river with its clear blue waters and underwater wonders. Discover the captivating Britania Islands, an archipelago of islets with white sand beaches and turquoise waters.

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and warm hospitality of our people as you explore Surigao del Sur. Experience our local traditions, savor our delicious cuisine, and create lasting memories in our beautiful province.

Welcome to Surigao del Sur, where natural beauty and adventure await you at every turn!
