Welcome to Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa, the capital of Palawan, offers a captivating blend of natural wonders and cultural experiences. While it serves as a gateway to popular destinations like El Nido and Coron, the city itself has much to offer to different types of travelers.

One of the highlights is the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site and home to the famous Underground River. Exploring this underground river on a guided boat tour allows you to witness the stunning limestone formations and unique ecosystem within the caverns.

For adventure enthusiasts, a visit to Ugong Rock Adventure is a must. This thrilling attraction offers activities such as zip-lining, trekking, and spelunking, all set against the beautiful backdrop of Puerto Princesa. It's a great opportunity to challenge yourself and immerse in the natural beauty of the area.

If you're looking for a beach escape, Puerto Princesa has splendid coastlines with pristine beaches. Whether you prefer relaxing by the shore or engaging in water sports, the beaches of Puerto Princesa offer a serene and picturesque setting.

Food lovers will also find delight in Puerto Princesa. The city is known for its seafood restaurants, where you can indulge in fresh and sumptuous feasts. From local specialties to international delights, you'll have plenty of dining options to satisfy your taste buds. Don't miss the chance to try authentic Vietnamese cuisine, as Puerto Princesa has a notable Vietnamese community.

When it comes to accommodation, Puerto Princesa offers a range of choices to suit various budgets and preferences. Whether you're planning a short stay or an extended vacation, you'll find luxury options as well as budget-friendly accommodations that cater to your needs.

With its natural wonders, cultural treasures, and delicious cuisine, Puerto Princesa promises a memorable experience for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation.
