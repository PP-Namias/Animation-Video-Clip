Welcome to Rizal

Rizal is a destination that seamlessly combines the beauty of nature with the conveniences of urban living. The province is characterized by lush hills, scenic valleys, and stunning views that stretch from the rugged foothills of Montalban and Antipolo to the picturesque shores of Laguna de Bay. This diverse terrain offers a wealth of exciting outdoor activities, including biking, mountain climbing, and camping amidst rolling hills, lakeshores, caves, and waterfalls.

Rizal is also renowned as the Cradle of Philippine Art, reflecting its rich artistic heritage. The province has been a breeding ground for generations of visual and performing artists, and you can witness the creative tradition come to life when exploring Rizal. Immerse yourself in the vibrant art scene by visiting local galleries in Angono, where you'll be amazed by the display of art masterpieces. A must-visit attraction is the Angono-Binangonan Petroglyphs, which showcases the oldest known art in the Philippines, dating back as far as 3000 BC. These ancient rock carvings provide a fascinating glimpse into the region's prehistoric past.

Aside from its artistic offerings, Rizal also offers a range of cultural experiences and historical landmarks. Explore the heritage sites in the towns of Morong and Baras, where you can visit centuries-old churches and ancestral houses that tell tales of Rizal's colonial past. Take a stroll along the streets lined with colorful houses in the artist community of Blanco Family Museum in Angono, and engage with local artisans and craftsmen who continue to preserve traditional arts and crafts.

For nature enthusiasts, Rizal provides ample opportunities to appreciate the province's natural wonders. Take a refreshing dip in the cool waters of Tinipak River or go on a hike to discover hidden waterfalls such as Daranak Falls and Batlag Falls. Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape from the summit of Mount Daraitan or Mount Pamitinan, both popular destinations for mountaineers and adventurers.

Rizal's close proximity to Metro Manila makes it an easily accessible getaway for those seeking a retreat into nature, culture, and art. Experience the best of both worlds as you immerse yourself in the province's natural beauty while still being within reach of modern amenities and urban comforts.

In summary, Rizal offers a harmonious blend of nature, art, culture, and history. Discover the province's scenic landscapes, explore its vibrant art scene, and indulge in outdoor adventures that will leave you with lasting memories.
